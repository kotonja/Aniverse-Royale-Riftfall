# Character Combat Spec

## Kairo Soljin

- Role: Vanguard
- Aura: Flame
- Feel: beginner-friendly brawler with strong damage and medium survivability.
- Strengths: simple engage, area burst, guard enhancer.
- Weaknesses: predictable range, moderate mobility.
- Base stats: 100 health, 100 shield, 100 flow, 100 guard, sprint 22.
- Passive: Solar Tempo placeholder.
- Basic: Flame Combo, 12 damage, short range.
- Skill 1: Ember Rush, forward rush, 22 damage, 5 second cooldown.
- Skill 2: Sunburst Guard, temporary damage reduction and guard restore, 8 second cooldown.
- Skill 3: Meteor Step, leap slam, 28 damage, 10 second cooldown.
- Ultimate: Dawn Breaker, large area solar burst, 42 damage, 28 second cooldown.
- VFX notes: orange, gold, bright solar pulses.
- Balance notes: should be strong at beginner brawling but not dominate range.

## Mira Gale

- Role: Skirmisher
- Aura: Gale
- Feel: fast aerial duelist with high movement expression.
- Strengths: speed, poke, repositioning.
- Weaknesses: lower health and guard.
- Base stats: 90 health, 100 shield, 110 flow, 90 guard, sprint 25.
- Passive: Slipstream placeholder.
- Basic: Wind Combo, 10 damage, fast cooldown.
- Skill 1: Razor Arc, mid-range slash, 18 damage, 5 second cooldown.
- Skill 2: Wind Step, mobility dash, light damage, 7 second cooldown.
- Skill 3: Cyclone Lift, area wind hit, 20 damage, 10 second cooldown.
- Ultimate: Tempest Waltz, storm burst, 36 damage, 28 second cooldown.
- VFX notes: cyan, white, wind rings.
- Balance notes: should escape and reposition, but not forever.

## Sora Nym

- Role: Support
- Aura: Light
- Feel: calm, bright, precise medic who still has a reliable poke.
- Strengths: shields, healing, reveal marks, team value.
- Weaknesses: lower damage, lower guard, needs allies to shine.
- Base stats: 95 health, 105 shield, 100 flow, 90 guard, sprint 23.
- Passive: Guiding Star. AR-003 grants small ultimate charge when Sora heals or shields. Ultimates are still cooldown-based, with a TODO for future charge conversion.
- Basic: Starlight Bolt Chain, 9 damage, reliable mid-range poke.
- Skill 1: Starlight Bolt, 16 damage, short LightMarked reveal, 5 second cooldown.
- Skill 2: Constellation Shield, 25 shield to closest nearby ally or self, 9 second cooldown.
- Skill 3: Star Mend, 18 ally heal or 10 self heal, 11 second cooldown. Future AR-004 revive beam hook.
- Ultimate: Nova Sanctuary, 8 second star field, pulses 4 heal and 3 shield to allies, 30 second cooldown.
- VFX notes: light gold, soft white, star circles, short reveal marks.
- Balance notes: support should matter without outhealing all damage forever.

## Atlas Null

- Role: Tank / Controller
- Aura: Gravity
- Feel: heavy, calm, space-controlling frontliner.
- Strengths: guard, control, damage reduction, zone pressure.
- Weaknesses: slower movement, telegraphed ultimate.
- Base stats: 115 health, 110 shield, 90 flow, 120 guard, sprint 20.
- Passive: Heavy Center. AR-003 reduces Atlas knockback and slightly improves guard regeneration.
- Basic: Gravity Palm Chain, 13 damage, high guard damage, slight pull.
- Skill 1: Pull Palm, gravity pulse, 14 damage, pulls enemies, 7 second cooldown.
- Skill 2: Orbit Guard, 35 percent self damage reduction and 15 percent nearby ally reduction, 5 second duration, 12 second cooldown.
- Skill 3: Heavy Step, 18 damage, 3 second slow field, 11 second cooldown.
- Ultimate: Blackstar Drop, 1.2 second warning, 40 damage if enemies stay in area, 32 second cooldown.
- VFX notes: purple, black-blue, gravity rings, dark warning circle.
- Balance notes: should be hard to displace, not unkillable.
